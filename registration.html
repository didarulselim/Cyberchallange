Need Team Building Tips?

Team Making Suggestion

The team should be composed with members having following identical skills:

  • Expertise on Problem Solving
  • Expertise on Related Programming Skill
  • Expertise on UX (UI)
  • Expertise on Presentation
  • Good Team Player
  • Smart Decision Maker

Your team should be led by a decisive minded decision maker, who will lead the team and delegate the problem solving related task to his problem solver and programmer, who will guide the UX designer for a good user experience for the developed solution. All team members should have minimum presentation skill and also team working spirit.

Apply Instruction

Following Information will be Required to Apply:

  1. Personal Information of each Team Member.
  2. NID Card Number and Scanned Copy of NID (for professionals) or Student ID Number and Scanned Copy of Student ID (for students who do not have NID) each Team Member.
  3. Authorization Letter in organization letter pad signed by Company Manager or Department Heads.
  4. Photograph Of each member(Size: 200*200, Max 500KB)


Conditions:

  • One organization (Company/University) cannot nominate two (2) teams in one track (web or mobile) but can nominate two (2) teams in two tracks (web & mobile). For example, ABC Co. or ABC University can send team A for Web and team B for Mobile.
  • Professionals nominated by companies and students nominated by universities should be the professionals or students of present. NO previous employees or students are allowed. Employees and Students should be able to submit necessary documents in favor of this condition as and when asked.
  • Each Team should consist 4 members
  • We will provide Internet Connection, Supplies (i.e. Pen, Paper, Pencil etc.), and Food in our premises, BUT you should carry your necessary gadgets.
  • Remember, Judges are seasoned professionals in the respective tracks and are free of any biasness, so Judges Decisions are final.
