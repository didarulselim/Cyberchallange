Following Information will be Required to Apply:

  1. Personal Information of each Team Member.

  2. NID Card Number and Scanned Copy of NID (for professionals) or Student ID Number and Scanned Copy of Student ID (for students who do not have NID) each Team Member.

  3. Authorization Letter in organization letter pad signed by Company Manager or Department Heads.

  4. Photograph Of each member(Size: 200*200, Max 500KB)

Conditions:

  1. One organization (Company/University) cannot nominate two (2) teams in one track (web or mobile) but can nominate two (2) teams in two tracks (web & mobile). For example, ABC Co. or ABC University can send team A for Web and team B for Mobile.

  2. Professionals nominated by companies and students nominated by universities should be the professionals or students of present. NO previous employees or students are allowed. Employees and Students should be able to submit necessary documents in favor of this condition as and when asked.

  3. Each Team should consist 4 members

  4. We will provide Internet Connection, Supplies (i.e. Pen, Paper, Pencil etc.), and Food in our premises, BUT you should carry your necessary gadgets.

  5. Remember, Judges are seasoned professionals in the respective tracks and are free of any biasness, so Judges Decisions are final.
